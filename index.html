OUR FOUNDER

Barr. Ifeanyi Eleazu is the founder and director of Aldom Construction

Quality and Integrity are the core values upon which Ifeanyi Eleazu founded ALDOM Construction in 1990.

A native of Nigeria, Ifeanyi built and developed building projects across the South and other parts of Nigeria. He has headed multi-million naira projects in Asaba, Anambra, Warri and other parts of Nigeria.

Today, Ifeanyi combines experience and knowledge with a commitment to quality and honest communication to do the best job possible for every ALDOM client.
